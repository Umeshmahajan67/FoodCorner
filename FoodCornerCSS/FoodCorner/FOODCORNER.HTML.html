Indian Pakora recipe is my favorite recipe . and i know how to make it!. Classic Indian Pakoras are made with sliced onions coated in a spicy chickpea batter. They are spicy and a very common snack eaten during rainy seasons in India. You only need super simple ingredients to make this delicious snack.

Ingredients To Make Pakora:-

  1. 🍅 Chickpea flour and gram flour (besan)
  2. 🧅 Chopped onions
  3. 🌶 Green chilies (optional)
  4. 🥛 Water
  5. 🏺 Spices - red chili powder, paprika, coriander seeds
  6. 🧂 Baking Soda
  7. 🧂 salt

How to make pakora

Making these Classic Pakoras is super simple and only requires two steps:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients and combine it with water to reach the right consistency.
    • Add all dry ingredients in a bowl: Add the chickpea or gram flour, spices, salt, coriander seeds and onions into a bowl and mix them well.
    • Add water to make a batter: Add enough water to make a thick batter that still holds its shape as in the picture above. You’re now ready to move on to step two.
  2. Fry till golden-brown: Fry the pakoras until they are crunchy on the outside and turn golden-brown in color. Take them out and place them on a kitchen paper towel so the excess oil is removed.

Serve with your favorite sauce, dip or chutney!
